About Us

Dr.Vatsala is a classical and light music singer, song writer, composer and a music teacher . I started training in Hindustani music at the age of eight, under the guidance of guru smt: Anuradha Deshapande and guru Mr. Iswar Gadibidi Further received my bachelor degree in music from maharani's Arts college and MA music from Bangalore University and then pursued my music education from the eminent gurus Vid Dr-Sarvamangala Shankar, Mysore Nagamani Shrinath, Padma shankar, Nagaratna Shiva Shankar, Dr. Susheela, Dr Hamsini Nagendra, Dr Nagavalli Nagaraj, Dr B M Jayashree etc. I have won several prizes during my student life and received Doctorate for the thesis "Music In South Indian Inscriptions" under the guidance of Dr. Sarvamangala Shankar, Bangalore University "Ranga Prapancha Rashtra Prashasti" honored her in the year 2012.

I have travelled extensively all over Karnataka & India Presenting music performances and represented Karnataka during her collage days for the National Integration camp, sponsored by Ministry of Culture, Youth Affairs and sports, Govt of India During this camp she attended music workshops by legendary musicians like Late Shri Jagjith Singh ji, Ila arun ji etc and trained ITC Bengaluru Factory ladies for choir during ITC 100 year celebration and worked as judge for many prestigious music competitions Dr.Vatsala served 11 years as Head of the Department of music at Presidency School kasturi nagar and a dedicated teacher with a vast group of disciples across the globe Students From USA, UK, Sweden and all over India are learning music under her guidance Presently she is running her own music academy in the name of Dr.Vatsala's Sangeetalaya This book was specially designed to make beginners music learning easy.
